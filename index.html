Undergraduate Internships

Gain real-world accounting experience while still in school. Our undergraduate internship program is designed for college accounting students who want to develop professional skills and make an impact at B.R. Hornung.

Program Overview

Work alongside experienced accountants on real client engagements. You'll gain hands-on experience in tax preparation, audit, and accounting services while earning competitive compensation.

Requirements

  • Currently enrolled in an accredited college/university accounting program
  • Minimum 3.0 GPA (preferred)
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Excel and accounting software
  • Strong attention to detail and communication skills

What We Offer

  • Competitive hourly wage
  • Flexible scheduling to work around your academic calendar
  • Mentorship from CPA professionals
  • Potential for full-time employment upon graduation
